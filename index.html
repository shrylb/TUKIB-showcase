About TUKIB

TUKIB is a comprehensive system designed specifically for the University of the Philippines Visayas (UPV) Regional Research Center. It serves as a centralized hub for managing research facilities, services, and user interactions.

Built with the developers' blood, sweat tears, and user-centric design, TUKIB streamlines the research process by providing easy access to laboratory resources, service bookings, and administrative tools. The platform supports multiple user roles and provides tailored experiences for both researchers and administrators.

Our mission is to enhance research productivity and collaboration by providing a seamless digital experience that connects researchers with the resources they need to advance scientific discovery and innovation.
